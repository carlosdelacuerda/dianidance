Our Dance Styles

Afrobeat

Afrobeat is a dynamic and expressive dance style rooted in African culture, blending traditional movements with modern urban influences. It is characterized by rhythmic footwork, body isolations, and powerful musicality that reflects the energy and diversity of African music. Afrobeat is not just a dance, but a form of expression that connects movement with rhythm and attitude.

At Diani Dance, Afrobeat classes focus on coordination, groove, and confidence. You will learn how to move freely, follow the rhythm, and express yourself through energetic combinations adapted to all levels. In the vibrant setting of Diani, Afrobeat becomes an exciting and liberating experience that combines fitness, culture, and pure enjoyment.

Twerking

Twerking is a high-energy dance style with roots in African dance traditions, later popularized globally through hip-hop culture. It focuses on controlled hip movements, isolations, and rhythm, requiring both technique and body awareness. Twerking combines strength, coordination, and musicality, creating a powerful and expressive way to connect with the music.

At Diani Dance, Twerking classes emphasize technique, control, and confidence. You will learn fundamental movements step by step, improving coordination and body control while building strength and endurance. In a supportive and fun environment, Twerking becomes an empowering experience that helps you gain confidence, express yourself, and enjoy movement without limits.

Kizomba

Kizomba originated in Angola, influenced by traditional African rhythms and later shaped by Caribbean and Portuguese musical elements. Known for its smooth, grounded movements and close partner connection, Kizomba is a dance built on subtlety, control, and musical interpretation. The steps are precise yet soft, creating a flowing sensation that matches the steady and melodic rhythm of the music.

At Diani Dance, Kizomba classes focus on posture, connection, and refined technique. You will learn how to move with control and confidence while understanding the structure of the music and the importance of leading and following with clarity. In the warm and welcoming environment of Diani, Kizomba offers a calm yet powerful dance experience that highlights connection, rhythm, and elegance.

Salsa

With deep African roots expressed through rhythm, percussion, and body movement, Salsa is a vibrant dance that evolved in the Caribbean, particularly in Cuba, before expanding worldwide. It blends Afro-Caribbean musical structures with Spanish melodies, creating a dynamic dialogue between music and movement. Salsa is characterized by energetic footwork, fluid turns, partner connection, and a strong sense of timing that makes every song an invitation to move.

At Diani Dance, Salsa classes focus on technique, musicality, and confident partner work. You will learn essential steps, combinations, and how to interpret the music naturally, whether you are a complete beginner or looking to refine your style. In the relaxed and inspiring atmosphere of Diani, Salsa becomes more than a dance — it is a social experience that connects people through rhythm and joy.

Bachata

Rooted in African rhythms and shaped in the Dominican Republic, Bachata has evolved significantly over time. Bachata Sensual is a modern evolution of traditional Dominican Bachata, incorporating smoother transitions, body waves, isolations, and a stronger emphasis on fluid partner connection. While it respects its origins, this contemporary style introduces circular movements and expressive interpretation of melody and lyrics.

At Diani Dance, Bachata Sensual classes emphasize technique, body control, and clear leading and following skills. You will develop balance, coordination, and musical awareness while learning how to move in harmony with your partner. Set in the beautiful surroundings of Diani, Bachata becomes an elegant and immersive experience, combining rhythm, connection, and expression in every step.

Events and Social Dancing

At Diani Dance, learning goes beyond the lessons themselves. We attend social dance events together with our students, giving them the opportunity to experience the true atmosphere of Salsa, Bachata, and Kizomba nights. These outings allow participants to step onto the dance floor, enjoy the music, and immerse themselves in the local social dance scene.

During each event, a Diani Dance instructor accompanies the group to provide guidance and support. The teacher helps students identify and correct mistakes, improve timing, refine technique, and build confidence while dancing with different partners. Having a professional present in a real social setting helps students feel supported and progress more effectively.

Participation in these social dance nights is completely optional, but highly recommended for those who want to gain more confidence and real experience. Students can put into practice what they have learned in class, adapt to different partners, and develop musicality and connection in a natural way. At the same time, they meet new people, expand their network, and become part of a vibrant dance community. These outings add an extra dimension to the learning journey, combining progress, connection, and enjoyment.
