Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described below. This website operates internationally but is based in Kenya.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to collect anonymous information about website usage, such as page visits, time spent on the site, and traffic sources. This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) for analytics, and data is anonymized.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to measure website performance and traffic. GA4 may set cookies on your device. No personal data is collected without your consent.

4. Purpose of Cookies

Cookies are used to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly.
  • Understand how visitors interact with the website.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. You can block or delete cookies at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

6. Consent

For users located in the European Union, we request consent before using analytics cookies. You can accept or decline cookies through the banner displayed when you first visit the website.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact
