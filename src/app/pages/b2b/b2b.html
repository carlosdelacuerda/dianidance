Custom Events & Experiences

We collaborate with hotels, resorts, beach clubs, villas, and travel agencies to design and deliver customized dance experiences adapted to your audience and brand positioning. Our services can be integrated into your existing activities or created as exclusive events for your guests.

Options include group dance classes, private sessions, social dance events with professional dancers acting as animators, and live dance shows performed by experienced artists. Each experience is designed to be engaging, interactive, and memorable, helping your guests connect, have fun, and enjoy a unique atmosphere.

All events are flexible and can be adapted in terms of style, duration, group size, and location within Diani or surrounding areas. We handle the full experience, ensuring professionalism, quality, and a seamless integration with your service offering.

Partnership & Commission Model

We offer collaboration opportunities for businesses that want to provide added value to their clients while generating additional revenue. Hotels, resorts, travel agencies, tour operators, and accommodation providers can partner with us by recommending our dance experiences to their guests.

Through this model, your business earns a commission for every client referred to Diani Dance. We take care of the full service delivery, ensuring a high-quality experience that reflects positively on your brand while creating an additional income stream for your business.

This partnership is simple, flexible, and scalable. It allows you to enhance your client offering without operational complexity, while giving your guests access to unique and memorable activities during their stay in Diani.
